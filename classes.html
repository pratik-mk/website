Event

Annual Cultural Program.

We in Shreekrishna Nrithalayam Nagpur organize annual function of our institute every year where all our students participate.The main attraction of our annual function is the Dance Drama.Some of our dance dramas are Krishna-amrutham,Streeshakti,Shiva-shakti,Navarasakulamai.

Event

Cultural Harmony (competition).

We organize All India Dance and music Competition -Culrural Harmony every year where the participants are from all over the country. We also organize Krishnamayookham Dance Festival where we select artists to perform Our motto is to Promote Indian Art and Culture.Students in Shreekrishna Nrithalayam appear for exams every year for Gandharva Mahavidyala ,Mumbai and Hindustan art s and Music society,Kolkata.

krishnamyookham Dance festival.

The biggest dance festival of Orange City "KRISHNAMAYOOKHAM DANCE FESTIVAL" which is an indispensable part of the CULTURA HARMONY organised by SHREEKRISHNA CULTURAL FOUNDATION & SHREEKRISHNA NRITHALAYAM. In this mega event many national and international exponents have staged their thumping performances.
